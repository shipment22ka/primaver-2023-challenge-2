Primavera

Primavera is a coding competition on Khan Academy, with six teams each having its own avatar and coders ranging from novice to "legend" (for people who think they're better than they are, we do have a so called "legend" on our team so just don't tell him or he might be offended), consisting of a one week homepage challenge, another two week long challenges, a three week coding marathon with three mini-challenges, and a ten day grand finale. You can sign up for it in this thread.
